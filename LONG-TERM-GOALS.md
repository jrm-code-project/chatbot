# LONG-TERM GOALS

These goals stay ordered by urgency. When `SHORT-TERM-GOALS.md` becomes empty, decompose the highest-priority unfinished item here into a fresh short-term goal list.

1. [ ] Add functionality that makes `chatbot` a superior chatbot suite. **(Current decomposition target)**
   - Expand the framework's capabilities, integrations, ergonomics, and reliability so it stands out as a strong general-purpose conversational toolkit.
2. [ ] Make `chatbot` a dependable research and experimentation platform.
   - Keep persona, backend, MCP, and tool flows reliable enough that they can support sustained scientific and engineering use across varied projects.
3. [ ] Keep the codebase maintainable as capability grows.
   - Continue reducing oversized modules, duplicated orchestration, and brittle tests so future work stays cheap and safe.
